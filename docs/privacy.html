AnyListen — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Summary: AnyListen collects no data of any kind. All audio stays on your device. The app contains no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, no accounts, and it never connects to the internet.

Microphone audio

AnyListen routes live microphone audio directly to your chosen audio output (headphones, hearing aids, or speakers) in real time. Microphone audio is never recorded, never stored, and never transmitted off your device. When you stop listening, audio processing stops.

Data we collect

None. AnyListen does not collect, use, share, or sell any personal data or usage data. There are no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in the app, so no third party receives any information about you.

On-device settings

Your preferences (chosen input, monitor volume, and the two Settings toggles) are stored locally on your device using iOS preferences storage. They never leave your device and are deleted when you delete the app.

Data retention, deletion, and consent

Because no data leaves your device, there is nothing retained elsewhere and nothing to delete on our side. You can revoke microphone permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone. Deleting the app removes its on-device settings. You can also contact us (below) with any privacy request.

Children

The app collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests: open an issue on the AnyListen GitHub repository.
